What I Think About DADDY

Daddy really likes math
He tells to do this and and that
He is'nt very fat and he loves to do python
and he gives our brains a marathon
He sometimes makes us yawn,
When we want to play pokemon,
He makes us do python.
By: Smayan and Heet
